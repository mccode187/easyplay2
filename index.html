Instructions

  1. Select "text" and type (or copy-paste) whitespace-separated notes into the box labelled "notes". Alternatively, select "file", upload a MIDI file, and select a track.
  2. Click start.
  3. Press keys to play notes!

Details

Each note consists of a pitch (required), an accidental (optional), and an octave (optional).

  • Pitches: c d e f g a b
  • Accidentals: # & ## && (default is natural / no accidental)
  • Octaves: 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8 (default is the most recent octave, or 4, at the beginning of the piece)

Example notes: c b3 b& c#4 a## g&&5

Links: demo mozart demo bach

Afterwards

Thanks for playing! I hope you enjoyed it!
